## Defect Lightning Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/defect-lightning-build

Channel Lightning orbs and boost them with Focus for devastating passive damage each turn. Electrodynamics turns every Lightning evoke into AoE. Echo Form doubles your best play each turn for exponential scaling.

### Core cards

- Electrodynamics
- Zap (`zap`)
- Defragment (`defragment`)
- Echo Form (`echo-form`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Defragment | Gain 1 Focus (2 upgraded). Every copy permanently buffs ALL orbs. Stack aggressively. |
| must_pick | Electrodynamics | Lightning now hits ALL enemies. Transforms single-target damage into room-clearing AoE. |
| must_pick | Echo Form | Play the first card each turn twice. Doubles Defragment, damage cards, everything. |
| high | Capacitor | Gain 2 Orb slots (3 upgraded). More slots = more passive Lightning damage. |
| high | Ball Lightning | Deal 7 damage + channel Lightning. Efficient damage + orb generation in one card. |
| high | Storm | Whenever you play a Power, channel 1 Lightning. Free orb generation. |
| high | Coolheaded | Channel Frost + draw 1 (2 upgraded). Defense + card draw. |
| medium | Subroutine | Innate Power. Channel 1 Lightning and 1 Frost. Consistent start to every fight. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Ball Lightning (`ball-lightning`)
- Storm (`storm`)
- Capacitor (`capacitor`)
- Subroutine (`subroutine`)
- Multi-Cast (`multi-cast`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Cracked Core** — Defect's starter. Channel 1 Lightning at start of combat. Free passive damage from turn 1.
- **Mummified Hand** — After playing a Power, a random card in hand costs 0 this turn. Essential for chaining Powers.
- **Inserter** — Gain an Orb slot every 2 turns. More slots = more passive Lightning orbs.

**Good**

- **Gold-Plated Cables** — Rightmost Orb triggers its passive an extra time. Double Lightning passive on your oldest orb.
- **Data Disk** — Start each combat with 1 Focus. Free Focus that makes every orb better immediately.
- **Runic Capacitor** — Start with 3 extra Orb slots. Immediately lets you fill up with Lightning orbs.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Power-heavy builds need to play many cards in setup turns. 6-card limit is painful.
- **Philosopher Stone** — +1 Strength to enemies is dangerous when you need time to set up orbs and Focus.
- **Cursed Key** — Curses dilute the deck, making it harder to draw Defragment and Echo Form.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Cracked Core** — At the start of each combat, Channel 1 Lightning.
- **Mummified Hand** — Whenever you play a Power, a random card in your Hand is free to play that turn.

## Defect Claw Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/defect-claw-build

The fan-favorite zero-cost spam deck that cycles through cheap attacks at blazing speed, with Claw's damage permanently ramping every time it's played. All for One retrieves all 0-cost cards from the discard pile for massive combo turns, while Feral doubles up 0-cost card plays. When the machine gets going, nothing can stop the Claw.

### Core cards

- Claw (`claw`)
- All for One (`all-for-one`)
- Scrape (`scrape`)
- Feral (`feral`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Claw | The build's namesake. 0 Energy, permanently ramps +2 damage per play. Take every copy — 3-4 Claws is ideal. |
| must_pick | All for One | Retrieves ALL 0-cost cards from discard pile. Enables cycling Claws multiple times per turn. |
| must_pick | Scrape | Draw 3, discard cards costing 2+. Filters hand to 0-cost cards and cycles through the deck rapidly. |
| must_pick | Feral | Power that plays every 0-cost card twice. Effectively doubles all Claw damage and triggers. |
| high | Hologram | Retrieves a card from discard. Fetches All for One or Scrape for another combo cycle. Upgrade removes Exhaust. |
| high | FTL | 0 Energy attack + draw a card if fewer than 4 cards in play. Keeps the chain going. |
| high | Go for the Eyes | 0 Energy Weak application. Reduces enemy damage while being a free Claw-compatible attack. |
| medium | Panache | Deals 10 AoE damage every 5th card played in a turn. With 10-20 cards per turn, triggers 2-4 times. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Go For The Eyes (`go-for-the-eyes`)
- Beam Cell (`beam-cell`)
- Momentum Strike (`momentum-strike`)
- FTL (`ftl`)
- Flash Of Steel (`flash-of-steel`)
- Hologram (`hologram`)
- Skim (`skim`)
- Panache (`panache`)
- Secret Weapon (`secret-weapon`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Unceasing Top** — Draw a card whenever your hand is empty. With all 0-cost cards, the deck goes infinite — play everything, draw more, repeat forever.
- **Nunchaku** — Gain 1 Energy after every 3 attacks. Claw spam triggers this constantly, generating free Energy for All for One.
- **Shuriken** — Gain 1 Strength after every 3 attacks. Each Strength point adds damage to every Claw hit. Scales explosively.

**Good**

- **Kunai** — Gain 1 Dexterity after every 3 attacks. Boosts Block from any defensive cards in the deck.
- **Ornamental Fan** — Gain 4 Block after every 3 attacks. Free defense while spamming Claws.
- **Iron Club** — Draw a card after every 3 attacks. Extra draw keeps the Claw chain going.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — 6 card limit per turn is catastrophic. The entire build relies on playing 10-20+ cards per turn.
- **Snecko Eye** — Randomizing costs on a deck full of 0-cost cards is strictly negative.
- **Cursed Key** — Curses bloat the deck and clog Scrape's filtering. Deck efficiency is critical.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Unceasing Top** — Whenever you have no cards in Hand during your turn, draw a card.
- **Nunchaku** — Every time you play 10 Attacks, gain 1 Energy.

| must_pick | Echo Form | Play the first card each turn twice. Double Creative AI's generated Power, double orb generation, double everything. |
| must_pick | Storm | Channel Lightning whenever you play a Power. Free orbs every time Creative AI triggers. |
| must_pick | Machine Learning | Draw 1 extra card per turn permanently. Essential for finding and playing generated Powers. |

| high | Capacitor | +2-3 Orb slots. Storm generates lots of Lightning orbs — need slots to hold them. |

**Best**
