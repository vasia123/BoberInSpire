## Necrobinder Osty Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/necrobinder-osty-build

Turn Osty into an unstoppable force. Stack Summon effects to build Osty's HP, then unleash devastating attacks through Reanimate or sacrifice for massive AoE. Osty acts as both your shield and your weapon.

### Core cards

- Dirge (`dirge`)
- Invoke (`invoke`)
- Borrowed Time (`borrowed-time`)
- Reanimate (`reanimate`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Dirge | Premium Summon card. Large HP boost to Osty with additional effects. |
| must_pick | Borrowed Time | Gain Energy per Summon. Turns Summon-heavy turns into explosive Energy generation. |
| must_pick | Reanimate | Deal damage equal to Osty's HP. Your primary win condition — 50 HP Osty = 50 damage hit. |
| must_pick | Invoke | Summon + Soul generation. Core engine card for both Osty and Soul resources. |
| high | Grave Warden | Block + Soul generation. The best common card for Necrobinder — take every copy. |
| high | Wisp | Cheap Summon source. Low cost means you can play multiple Summons per turn. |
| medium | Pull Aggro | Force all enemies to target Osty. Protects the Necrobinder when Osty has high HP. |
| medium | Sentry Mode | Osty attacks automatically. Turns Osty into an offensive weapon alongside being a shield. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Wisp (`wisp`)
- Grave Warden (`grave-warden`)
- Pull Aggro (`pull-aggro`)
- Sentry Mode (`sentry-mode`)
- Soul Storm (`soul-storm`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Bound Phylactery** — Necrobinder's starter relic. Ensures Osty persists and is resummoned automatically.
- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards turn 1. Find Summon cards faster to build Osty early.
- **Anchor** — Start with 10 Block. Extra protection while Osty is still small.

**Good**

- **Mummified Hand** — Powers cost 0 after playing a Power. Borrowed Time and Sentry Mode benefit greatly.
- **Meat on the Bone** — Heal 12 HP when below 50% after combat. Necrobinder has the lowest HP at 66.
- **Vajra** — +1 Strength buffs Reanimate and all attack damage.

**Avoid**

- **Philosopher Stone** — Enemies gain +1 Strength. Necrobinder's low HP (66) can't afford enemies hitting harder.
- **Ectoplasm** — Gold is crucial for purchasing Rare Summon cards and relics from shops.
- **Runic Dome** — Knowing enemy intent matters — you need to decide when Osty blocks vs when to Reanimate.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Bound Phylactery** — At the start of your turn, Summon 1.

## Necrobinder Soul Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/necrobinder-soul-build

A card-draw powerhouse that generates Souls (free 0-cost draw-2 Exhaust cards) and converts them into devastating damage via Soul Storm, Haunt, and Death March. By flooding the deck with Souls and playing them for massive draw, every turn becomes an explosive chain of card plays that overwhelm enemies with unstoppable accumulated damage.

### Core cards

- Haunt (`haunt`)
- Soul Storm (`soul-storm`)
- Death March (`death-march`)
- Capture Spirit (`capture-spirit`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Haunt | 6 damage to random enemy per Soul played. Multiple copies stack. 2 Haunts = 12 free damage per Soul. The #1 payoff card. |
| must_pick | Capture Spirit | 1 Energy for 3 Souls. The most efficient Soul generator. Take every copy offered. |
| must_pick | Soul Storm | Scaling finisher. +2 damage per Soul in exhaust pile. By mid-fight, easily deals 30-50+ damage per play. |
| must_pick | Death March | 8 damage +3 per card drawn this turn. With Souls drawing 2 each, this hits for 30-50+ damage reliably. |
| high | Grave Warden | 8 Block + 1 Soul for 1 Energy. Replaces Defend while feeding the Soul engine. Essential early pickup. |
| high | Dirge | X-cost: generates X Souls + X Summon for Osty. Excellent setup card that also strengthens your tank. |
| high | Devour Life | Summon 1 whenever you play a Soul. Turns every Soul into Osty growth, making Osty an invincible wall. |
| medium | Borrowed Time | Apply 3 Doom to yourself, gain 1 Energy. The Doom is nearly irrelevant when enemies die fast. Free Energy is excellent. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Grave Warden (`grave-warden`)
- Reave (`reave`)
- Dirge (`dirge`)
- Devour Life (`devour-life`)
- Soul Storm (`soul-storm`)
- Gold Axe (`gold-axe`)
- Borrowed Time (`borrowed-time`)
- Severance (`severance`)
- Glimpse Beyond (`glimpse-beyond`)
- Dredge (`dredge`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Funerary Mask** — Start every combat with 3 free Souls in draw pile. Accelerates the engine by an entire turn.
- **Pendulum** — Draw a card on every shuffle. The deck churns through cards so fast that this triggers 3-5+ times per combat.
- **Letter Opener** — Deal 5 AoE damage every 3 Skills played. All Souls count as Skills — triggers constantly.

**Good**

- **Dolly S Mirror** — Copy any card. Duplicating Haunt or Capture Spirit dramatically increases the deck's power.
- **Chemical X** — X-cost cards like Dirge gain +2 effect. 2 extra Souls and Summon per Dirge play.
- **Razor Tooth** — Upgrade every Attack and Skill played in combat. Souls count as Skills, and payoff cards upgrade to deal more damage.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — 6 card limit destroys this deck. Playing Souls alone can require 6+ card plays per turn.
- **Cursed Key** — Curses clog the draw pile and interfere with Soul draw timing.
- **Snecko Eye** — Randomizing costs breaks the Energy management. Souls are 0-cost by design; other cards have specific costs.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Funerary Mask** — At the start of each combat, add 3 Souls into your Draw Pile.
- **Pendulum** — Whenever you shuffle your Draw Pile, draw a card.

## Necrobinder Doom Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/necrobinder-doom-build

Apply Doom to enemies until it exceeds their HP, causing instant death. Bypasses all defenses, armor, and scaling. When Doom stacks equal or exceed enemy HP, they die at the end of their next turn. The ultimate inevitability.

### Core cards

- Capture Spirit (`capture-spirit`)
- Countdown (`countdown`)
- Grave Warden (`grave-warden`)
- Danse Macabre (`danse-macabre`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Capture Spirit | Apply Doom + generate Souls. The core Doom card that does everything the build needs. |
| must_pick | Countdown | Power that applies Doom passively each turn. Set-and-forget Doom generation. |
| must_pick | Danse Macabre | Every Soul generated applies 1 Doom to all enemies. Turns Soul engine into Doom engine. |
| must_pick | Grave Warden | Best common card. Block + Souls. The backbone of both defense and Soul generation. |
| high | Reave | Deal damage + generate Souls. Efficient Soul generation that also provides immediate offense. |
| high | Bodyguard | Starting deck Summon. Keeps Osty alive to tank while you stack Doom. |
| medium | Defile | Attack that applies Doom. Decent early-game Doom source with damage. |
| medium | Defy | Block card that synergizes with the Necrobinder's mechanics. Solid defense. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Defile (`defile`)
- Blight Strike (`blight-strike`)
- Reave (`reave`)
- Time's Up (`times-up`)
- End of Days (`end-of-days`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Bound Phylactery** — Necrobinder's starter relic. Osty absorbs damage while you focus on applying Doom.
- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards turn 1. Find Doom applicators and Soul generators quickly.
- **Anchor** — Start with 10 Block. Critical at 66 HP — buys time for Doom to accumulate.

**Good**

- **Mummified Hand** — Powers cost 0 after playing a Power. Countdown and Danse Macabre set up faster.
- **Tungsten Rod** — Reduce HP loss by 1. Every point matters at 66 base HP.
- **Meat on the Bone** — Heal 12 HP when below 50%. Essential survival for the Necrobinder's low HP pool.

**Avoid**

- **Philosopher Stone** — Enemies gain +1 Strength. At 66 HP, you can't afford enemies hitting harder while waiting for Doom.
- **Mark Of Pain** — Wounds in draw pile dilute the deck. Harder to find Doom applicators and Soul generators.
- **Cursed Key** — Curses dilute the deck just like Wounds. Doom build needs consistency above all.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Bound Phylactery** — At the start of your turn, Summon 1.

## Necrobinder Reaper Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/necrobinder-reaper-build

Transform into the Reaper and harvest enemies with devastating attack scaling. Reaper Form empowers your attacks with lifesteal and bonus damage, while The Scythe and Eradicate provide massive AoE. Unlike Soul or Doom builds, this is pure aggression — attack hard, heal through damage, and overwhelm enemies before they can respond.

### Core cards

- Reaper Form (`reaper-form`)
- The Scythe (`the-scythe`)
- Eradicate (`eradicate`)
- Lethality (`lethality`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Reaper Form | Lifesteal + bonus attack damage. The entire build's engine. Makes every attack heal you. |
| must_pick | The Scythe | Massive AoE damage. With Reaper Form lifesteal, it heals for each enemy hit. |
| must_pick | Eradicate | Heavy single-target removal. With Strength from Lethality, one-shots most enemies. |
| must_pick | Lethality | Gain Strength to amplify all attacks. The Scythe AoE with Strength is devastating. |
| high | Flatten | Damage + Summon for Osty. Keeps your tank alive while dealing damage. |
| high | Death March | Scaling damage per card drawn. Souls from Grave Warden boost this naturally. |
| medium | Grave Warden | 8 Block + 1 Soul. Defense and supplementary draw for the attack-heavy build. |
| medium | Necro Mastery | Enhances all attacks. Late-game power multiplier for The Scythe and Eradicate. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Blight Strike (`blight-strike`)
- Scourge (`scourge`)
- Flatten (`flatten`)
- Death March (`death-march`)
- Necro Mastery (`necro-mastery`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Bone Flute** — Necrobinder's starter relic. Enhances Osty interaction, providing better tanking while you set up Reaper Form.
- **Shuriken** — Gain 1 Strength every 3 Attacks. Amplifies The Scythe and Eradicate damage further.
- **Vajra** — +1 Strength at combat start. Every point of Strength is multiplied across The Scythe's AoE hits.

**Good**

- **Meat on the Bone** — Heal 12 HP if below 50% at end of combat. Insurance alongside Reaper Form's lifesteal.
- **Pen Nib** — Every 10th Attack deals double damage. Time with Eradicate for massive burst.
- **Akabeko** — First Attack each combat deals +8 damage. Strong opener when combined with Lethality.

**Avoid**

- **Philosopher Stone** — +1 Strength to enemies while you spend turns setting up Reaper Form. Too dangerous.
- **Velvet Choker** — Attack-heavy turns with Souls for draw may exceed 6 plays.
- **Runic Dome** — Need to know when to set up vs when to block. Reaper Form setup turns are vulnerable.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Bone Flute** — Whenever Osty attacks, gain 2 Block.
- **Shuriken** — Every time you play 3 Attacks in a single turn, gain 1 Strength.
