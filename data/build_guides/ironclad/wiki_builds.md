## Ironclad Strength Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/ironclad-strength-build

The classic Strength scaling build. Stack Strength through Inflame and Demon Form, then unleash devastating Heavy Blade attacks. One of the most consistent and beginner-friendly archetypes in Slay the Spire 2.

### Core cards

- Heavy Blade
- Inflame (`inflame`)
- Demon Form (`demon-form`)
- Offering (`offering`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Demon Form | Infinite Strength scaling. A single copy wins any long fight. |
| must_pick | Inflame | Immediate +2 Strength (3 upgraded). Core ramp card, take every copy offered. |
| must_pick | Heavy Blade | Primary win condition. Deals 14 + 3x Strength damage (5x upgraded). Scales absurdly. |
| must_pick | Offering | Lose 6 HP to gain 2 Energy and draw 5 cards. Enables explosive turns. |
| high | Fight Me! | Deals damage twice while gaining Strength. Strong early-mid game card. |
| high | Battle Trance | Draw 3 cards (4 upgraded). Helps find Heavy Blade and key pieces faster. |
| high | Shrug It Off | 8 Block + draw a card. Efficient defense that doesn't slow your deck. |
| medium | Flame Barrier | 12 Block + retaliatory damage. Solid defense that also deals damage to attackers. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Fight Me! (`fight-me`)
- Perfected Strike (`perfected-strike`)
- Battle Trance (`battle-trance`)
- Whirlwind (`whirlwind`)
- Bludgeon (`bludgeon`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Vajra** — Free +1 Strength from the start of every combat — perfect synergy.
- **Burning Blood** — Ironclad's starter relic. 6 HP heal per fight lets you skip resting and upgrade more.
- **Bag of Marbles** — Applies Vulnerable to all enemies turn 1, amplifying your Strength-boosted attacks.

**Good**

- **Ornamental Fan** — Free Block on every 3rd attack played — helps survive while ramping Strength.
- **Pen Nib** — Every 10th attack deals double damage. Devastating with a charged-up Heavy Blade.
- **Meat on the Bone** — Heal 12 HP when ending combat below 50% — extra survivability during Act 1-2 ramp phase.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Limits you to 6 cards per turn. Cripples turns where you need Offering + multiple attacks.
- **Runic Dome** — Not seeing enemy intent makes it hard to time defensive vs offensive turns.
- **Philosopher Stone** — +1 Energy is nice but +1 Strength to all enemies compounds with their existing damage.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Vajra** — Start each combat with 1 Strength.
- **Burning Blood** — At the end of combat, heal 6 HP.

## Ironclad Barricade Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/ironclad-barricade-build

A defensive powerhouse that converts Block into lethal damage. Barricade keeps your Block between turns while Body Slam deals damage equal to your current Block. The ultimate 'best defense is a good offense' strategy.

### Core cards

- Barricade (`barricade`)
- Body Slam (`body-slam`)
- Impervious (`impervious`)
- Shrug It Off (`shrug-it-off`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Barricade | The build's keystone. Block no longer decays at end of turn. Everything else depends on this. |
| must_pick | Body Slam | Deals damage equal to current Block. Your primary and often only win condition. |
| must_pick | Impervious | 30 Block for 2 Energy (40 upgraded). The single best Block card in the game. |
| must_pick | Shrug It Off | 8 Block + draw 1 card. Efficient Block that cycles your deck toward key pieces. |
| high | Stone Armor | Gain Plated Armor, adding passive Block every turn that Barricade preserves. |
| high | Second Wind | Exhaust all non-Attack cards in hand, gaining Block for each. Massive Block burst. |
| medium | True Grit | 7 Block and Exhaust a card. Thins your deck while adding Block. |
| medium | Iron Wave | 5 Block + 5 damage in one card. Versatile early pickup that does both jobs. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Iron Wave (`iron-wave`)
- Stone Armor (`stone-armor`)
- Crimson Mantle (`crimson-mantle`)
- Second Wind (`second-wind`)
- Colossus (`colossus`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Ornamental Fan** — Gain 4 Block every 3 attacks. Since Body Slam is an attack, this compounds your Block stack.
- **Anchor** — Start every combat with 10 Block. With Barricade, this Block persists and compounds.
- **Horn Cleat** — Gain 14 Block at the start of your 2nd turn. Free Block that stacks with Barricade.

**Good**

- **Thread And Needle** — Start combat with 4 Plated Armor. Passive Block every turn that Barricade preserves.
- **Meat on the Bone** — Heal 12 HP when ending combat below 50%. Helps in Act 1 before Barricade is found.
- **Orichalcum** — Gain 6 Block if you didn't Block this turn. Useful when drawing all attacks.

**Avoid**

- **Cursed Key** — Extra Energy is tempting but Curse cards dilute your deck, making it harder to draw Body Slam and Barricade.
- **Velvet Choker** — Block stacking requires playing many cards per turn. 6-card limit is crippling.
- **Runic Dome** — Knowing enemy intent is critical for deciding when to block vs when to Body Slam.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Ornamental Fan** — Every time you play 3 Attacks in a single turn, gain 4 Block.
- **Anchor** — Start each combat with 10 Block.

## Ironclad Exhaust Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/ironclad-exhaust-build

Leverage Exhaust synergies to turn your entire deck into an unstoppable engine. Every exhausted card generates Block, draws cards, and with Dead Branch, creates random replacements. Controlled chaos at its finest.

### Core cards

- Corruption (`corruption`)
- Dark Embrace (`dark-embrace`)
- Feel No Pain (`feel-no-pain`)
- Fiend Fire (`fiend-fire`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Corruption | Makes all Skills cost 0 but Exhaust. With Dark Embrace, every free Skill replaces itself. |
| must_pick | Dark Embrace | Draw 1 card whenever you Exhaust. Creates chain reactions with Corruption. |
| must_pick | Feel No Pain | Gain 3 Block (4 upgraded) per Exhaust. Provides massive passive defense. |
| high | Fiend Fire | Exhaust your entire hand, deal damage per card. The ultimate finisher for this build. |
| high | Offering | Draw 5 cards + 2 Energy. Fuels the engine on critical turns. |
| high | True Grit | 7 Block + Exhaust a card. Reliable defense and Exhaust trigger. |
| medium | Burning Pact | Exhaust 1 card, draw 2 (3 upgraded). Trades bad cards for better options. |
| medium | Havoc | Play the top card of your draw pile for free, then Exhaust it. More triggers. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- True Grit (`true-grit`)
- Burning Pact (`burning-pact`)
- Havoc (`havoc`)
- Brand (`brand`)
- Aggression (`aggression`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Dead Branch** — THE relic for this build. Every Exhausted card creates a random card in hand, making Corruption generate infinite value.
- **Burning Blood** — Ironclad's starter relic. The HP sustain is crucial while assembling the combo in Acts 1-2.
- **Strange Spoon** — 50% chance cards won't Exhaust. With Corruption, your Skills sometimes stay in hand — incredible with powerful Skills.

**Good**

- **Charon Ashes** — Whenever you Exhaust a card, deal 3 damage to all enemies. Free AoE just for doing what the build already does.
- **Mummified Hand** — Powers cost 0 after playing a Power. Huge for setting up Corruption + Dark Embrace + Feel No Pain.
- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards turn 1. More cards means more fuel for the Exhaust engine.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — This build wants to play 10+ cards per turn. A 6-card limit is catastrophic.
- **Snecko Eye** — Randomized costs interfere with Corruption making Skills free. High-cost random assignments waste turns.
- **Runic Pyramid** — Retaining hand between turns reduces new draws. This build wants fresh cards every turn to Exhaust.

## Ironclad Bloodletting Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/ironclad-bloodletting-build

A high-risk, high-reward self-damage archetype that converts HP loss into Strength via Rupture, then unleashes devastating attacks. Cards that deal damage to the Ironclad are generally more powerful than average, and this build turns that downside into a Strength-scaling machine.

### Core cards

- Rupture (`rupture`)
- Inferno (`inferno`)
- Bloodletting (`bloodletting`)
- Tear Asunder (`tear-asunder`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Rupture | Foundation of the build. Each self-damage instance grants permanent Strength. Upgrade doubles efficiency. |
| must_pick | Inferno | Deals AoE damage equal to HP lost this combat. Scales naturally with the self-damage strategy. |
| must_pick | Tear Asunder | Multi-hit finisher that gains additional strikes for each time you've lost HP. Nuclear with high Strength. |
| high | Crimson Mantle | Guaranteed 1 HP loss per turn triggers Rupture passively. Also provides 8 Block (10 upgraded) every turn. |
| high | Bloodletting | Lose 3 HP to gain 2 Energy. Only good with draw support but enables explosive combo turns. |
| high | Breakthrough | Strong AoE attack that only costs 1 HP. Best early-game damage card for this archetype. |
| medium | Brand | Free exhaust that also triggers 1 HP loss. Good for deck thinning and Rupture triggers. |
| medium | Feed | Gain max HP on kill. Offsets self-damage costs and gives a bigger HP pool to work with. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Hemokinesis (`hemokinesis`)
- Breakthrough (`breakthrough`)
- Crimson Mantle (`crimson-mantle`)
- Feed (`feed`)
- Offering (`offering`)
- Brand (`brand`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Self-Forming Clay** — Gain Block whenever you lose HP in combat — perfect synergy with self-damage cards.
- **Demon Tongue** — Works amazingly with Bloodletting and Offering, reducing the effective HP cost.
- **Centennial Puzzle** — Free draw on the first time you take damage each combat — triggers reliably in this build.

**Good**

- **Burning Blood** — Ironclad's starter relic. 6 HP heal per fight is essential to sustain self-damage costs.
- **Vajra** — Free +1 Strength stacks on top of Rupture scaling.
- **Meat on the Bone** — Heal 12 HP when ending combat below 50% — likely in a self-damage deck.

**Avoid**

- **Runic Dome** — You need to see enemy intent to decide when it's safe to lose more HP.
- **Philosopher Stone** — Enemies gaining +1 Strength compounds with the damage you're already taking from your own cards.
- **Coffee Dripper** — Losing the ability to rest is dangerous when your build drains your own HP.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Self-Forming Clay** — Whenever you lose HP in combat, gain 3 Block next turn.
- **Burning Blood** — At the end of combat, heal 6 HP.

## Ironclad Strike Build (B-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/ironclad-strike-build

A straightforward offensive deck that utilizes cards containing 'Strike' to power up Perfected Strike for massive single-hit damage. Easy to build with minimal synergy requirements, making it an excellent choice for beginners or when other archetypes don't come together.

### Core cards

- Perfected Strike (`perfected-strike`)
- Twin Strike (`twin-strike`)
- Pommel Strike (`pommel-strike`)
- Hellraiser (`hellraiser`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Perfected Strike | The build's core card. Minimum 18 damage at game start, scaling to 30+ with more Strike cards added. |
| must_pick | Hellraiser | Auto-plays Strike cards when drawn. Transforms the deck from manual beatdown to automated damage machine. |
| high | Twin Strike | 10 damage for 1 Energy, benefits from Strength on each hit. Counts as a Strike for Perfected Strike. |
| high | Pommel Strike | Decent damage plus card draw. The draw can trigger Hellraiser chains for extra free damage. |
| high | Expect a Fight | Extra Energy generation is critical when Perfected Strike costs 2 Energy per play. |
| medium | Colossus | Supplementary defensive tool. This deck otherwise lacks Block, so Colossus fills the gap. |
| medium | Taunt | Upgraded provides 2 Vulnerable. Playing this before Perfected Strike adds significant burst damage. |
| medium | Pyre | Extra Energy gives flexibility to play Perfected Strike and still have Energy for defense. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Breakthrough (`breakthrough`)
- Expect a Fight (`expect-a-fight`)
- Taunt (`taunt`)
- Inflame (`inflame`)
- Colossus (`colossus`)
- Pyre (`pyre`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Strike Dummy** — Obvious synergy — increases damage of all Strike cards. The perfect relic for this build.
- **Bag of Marbles** — Vulnerable on all enemies turn 1 makes your Perfected Strike hits even bigger.
- **Pen Nib** — Every 10th attack deals double damage. Devastating when it lines up with Perfected Strike.

**Good**

- **Vajra** — +1 Strength benefits every Strike card, especially multi-hit ones like Twin Strike.
- **Ornamental Fan** — Free Block every 3 attacks. Helps shore up this deck's defensive weakness.
- **Intimidating Helmet** — Extra Block on turns you play expensive cards like Perfected Strike.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Limits cards per turn, which conflicts with Hellraiser chains and multi-Strike turns.
- **Runic Dome** — This deck lacks good defense — you need to see enemy intent to know when to Block.
- **Cursed Key** — Adding Curses dilutes the deck, reducing Perfected Strike consistency and clogging draws.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Strike Dummy** — Cards containing "Strike" deal 3 additional damage.
- **Burning Blood** — At the end of combat, heal 6 HP.

## Ironclad Self-Wound Build (B-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/ironclad-self-wound-build

Turn self-inflicted damage into a devastating advantage. Rupture converts every HP lost into permanent Strength, while Tear Asunder gains extra hits for each HP lost in combat. A high-risk, high-reward archetype that weaponizes Ironclad's massive HP pool.

### Core cards

- Tear Asunder (`tear-asunder`)
- Rupture (`rupture`)
- Brand (`brand`)
- Offering (`offering`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Rupture | Gain 1 Strength whenever you lose HP from a card. The entire build's engine. Take immediately. |
| must_pick | Tear Asunder | 5 damage + extra hit per HP lost this combat. Primary damage payoff that scales with self-damage. |
| must_pick | Offering | Lose 6 HP for 2 Energy + draw 5. Triggers Rupture and enables explosive turns. |
| high | Brand | 0 cost: Exhaust 1 card, lose 1 HP, gain 1 Strength. Deck trimming + Rupture trigger + Strength. |
| high | Bloodletting | Lose 3 HP, gain 2 Energy. Triggers Rupture and funds expensive plays. |
| medium | Hemokinesis | Lose 2 HP, deal 15 damage. Solid damage that also triggers Rupture. |
| medium | Feed | Deal 10 damage. If it kills, gain 3 max HP permanently. Sustain for a build that loses HP frequently. |
| medium | Flame Barrier | 12 Block + retaliatory damage. Critical defense for a build with consistently low HP. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Bloodletting (`bloodletting`)
- Hemokinesis (`hemokinesis`)
- Inferno (`inferno`)
- Crimson Mantle (`crimson-mantle`)
- Feed (`feed`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Self-Forming Clay** — Gain 3 Block next turn after losing HP. Since you lose HP constantly, this provides free passive defense every turn.
- **Demon Tongue** — At the end of your turn, if you lost HP this turn, gain 1 Strength. Stacks with Rupture for double Strength gain.
- **Centennial Puzzle** — Draw 3 cards whenever you lose HP. Free card draw on a build that loses HP every turn.

**Good**

- **Meat on the Bone** — Heal 12 HP if below 50% at end of combat. Insurance against aggressive self-damage.
- **Burning Blood** — Ironclad's starter relic. 6 HP heal per fight helps offset the constant self-damage.
- **Vajra** — +1 Strength at combat start. Multiplied by Tear Asunder's many hits.

**Avoid**

- **Philosopher Stone** — Enemies gain +1 Strength while you're already at low HP. Too dangerous.
- **Runic Dome** — You need to know when enemies hit hard because your HP is often low. Blind intent is suicidal.
- **Cursed Key** — Opening chests costs 1 HP + adds Curses. Extra HP loss without Rupture benefit, and Curses bloat the deck.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Self-Forming Clay** — Whenever you lose HP in combat, gain 3 Block next turn.
- **Demon Tongue** — The first time you lose HP on your turn, heal HP equal to the amount lost.
