## Silent Poison Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/silent-poison-build

Stack Poison on enemies and watch them melt. Noxious Fumes provides passive scaling while you focus on survival. Catalyst doubles or triples Poison stacks for explosive burst damage. The most reliable Silent archetype.

### Core cards

- Noxious Fumes (`noxious-fumes`)
- Deadly Poison (`deadly-poison`)
- Bouncing Flask (`bouncing-flask`)
- Burst (`burst`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Noxious Fumes | Passive 2 Poison per turn (3 upgraded) to ALL enemies. The foundation of every Poison build. |
| must_pick | Burst | Play your next Skill twice. Burst + Catalyst = 6x Poison. Burst + Bouncing Flask = double AoE Poison. |
| must_pick | Deadly Poison | Apply 5 Poison (7 upgraded) for 1 Energy. Most efficient single-target Poison applicator. |
| high | Bouncing Flask | Apply 3 Poison to a random enemy 3 times. Solves the AoE problem and stacks fast. |
| high | Footwork | Gain 2 Dexterity (3 upgraded). Makes every Block card more efficient while Poison works. |
| high | Backflip | 5 Block + draw 2 cards. Excellent defense that also digs for Catalyst. |
| medium | Leg Sweep | Apply 2 Weak + 11 Block. Survivability card that buys time for Poison. |
| medium | Malaise | Spend all Energy to apply that much Weak and remove that much Strength. Boss killer. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Poisoned Stab (`poisoned-stab`)
- Footwork (`footwork`)
- Leg Sweep (`leg-sweep`)
- Malaise (`malaise`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Ring of the Snake** — Silent's starter relic. Draw 2 extra cards turn 1 to find key Poison pieces fast.
- **Snecko Skull** — Gain 1 Poison on enemies when you apply Poison. Amplifies every Poison source in the deck.
- **Specimen** — When a Poisoned enemy dies, transfer their Poison to a random enemy. Incredible in multi-fights.

**Good**

- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards at start of combat. More chances to find Noxious Fumes and Catalyst early.
- **Kunai** — Gain 1 Dexterity every 3 attacks. Better Block generation while Poison does the work.
- **Tungsten Rod** — Reduce HP loss by 1. Helpful when enemies chip through your defense while waiting for Poison to kill.

**Avoid**

- **Philosopher Stone** — +1 Energy is nice but +1 Strength to enemies means they hit harder while you wait for Poison.
- **Ectoplasm** — Can't gain Gold. Gold is important for Silent to buy key cards and relics from shops.
- **Mark Of Pain** — Wounds added to draw pile dilute your deck, making it harder to draw Catalyst and key pieces.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Ring of the Snake** — At the start of each combat, draw 2 additional cards.
- **Snecko Skull** — Whenever you apply Poison, apply an additional 1 Poison.

## Silent Sly Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/silent-sly-build

A fast-cycling deck that exploits the Silent's unique Sly keyword to play powerful cards for free when discarded. By rapidly cycling through a thin deck with discard effects, Sly cards trigger automatically, generating massive value without spending Energy. One of the most powerful and skill-intensive Silent archetypes.

### Core cards

- Acrobatics (`acrobatics`)
- Untouchable (`untouchable`)
- Tactician (`tactician`)
- Tools of the Trade (`tools-of-the-trade`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Tools of the Trade | Guarantees a free discard (Sly trigger) every turn. The engine that makes the deck consistent. |
| must_pick | Tactician | Sly Energy generator. When discarded, grants 1 Energy (2 upgraded). Fuels more card plays each turn. |
| must_pick | Acrobatics | Primary draw + discard engine. Draw 3 (4 upgraded), discard 1 to trigger Sly. Essential cycling tool. |
| must_pick | Untouchable | Core defensive Sly card. When discarded, provides free Block. Your primary survival tool. |
| high | Prepared | 0 Energy discard enabler. Draws 1, discards 1. Free Sly activation every time. |
| high | Calculated Gamble | Replace entire hand and trigger ALL Sly cards. Devastating combo potential on key turns. |
| high | Speedster | Deals damage while cycling. Provides consistent offensive output in the Sly engine. |
| medium | Flick-Flack | Strong damage card that's even better when triggered by Sly discard. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Flick Flack (`flick-flack`)
- Speedster (`speedster`)
- Calculated Gamble (`calculated-gamble`)
- Reflex (`reflex`)
- Haze (`haze`)
- Master Planner (`master-planner`)
- Serpent Form (`serpent-form`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Tingsha** — Deals damage whenever you discard a card. In a Sly deck, you discard constantly — this adds up to massive free damage.
- **Tough Bandages** — Gain Block whenever you discard a card. Combined with constant Sly discards, provides excellent passive defense.
- **The Abacus** — Gain Block whenever you shuffle your draw pile. A thin cycling deck shuffles frequently, stacking free Block.

**Good**

- **Nunchaku** — Gain Energy after every 3 attacks played. Sly attack cards count, making this trigger frequently.
- **Ornamental Fan** — Gain Block after every 3 attacks. Sly attacks trigger this, providing extra defense.
- **Pendulum** — Draw a card on shuffle. A fast-cycling deck shuffles often, giving more draw opportunities.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Limits to 6 cards per turn. Sly cards count as plays, so this severely limits the deck's cycling potential.
- **Snecko Eye** — Randomizing costs conflicts with the precise Energy management this deck requires.
- **Cursed Key** — Adding Curses bloats the deck and blocks cycling efficiency.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Tingsha** — Whenever you discard a card during your turn, deal 3 damage to a random enemy for each card discarded.
- **Tough Bandages** — Whenever you discard a card during your turn, gain 3 Block.

## Silent Grand Finale Infinite Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/silent-grand-finale-build

An infinite combo build that cycles the entire deck to repeatedly play Grand Finale, dealing 50 damage to all enemies each loop. By keeping the deck extremely thin and using discard/draw cards to empty the draw pile, Grand Finale can be played multiple times per turn for devastating AoE damage that trivializes every encounter.

### Core cards

- Grand Finale (`grand-finale`)
- Acrobatics (`acrobatics`)
- Calculated Gamble (`calculated-gamble`)
- Tactician (`tactician`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Grand Finale | The win condition. 50 AoE damage when draw pile is empty. Take every copy — multiple copies mean multiple plays per cycle. |
| must_pick | Acrobatics | Draw 3, discard 1. Primary cycle tool and Sly activator. Upgraded draws 4 for even faster cycling. |
| must_pick | Calculated Gamble | Replace entire hand. Instantly empties draw pile when timed correctly. Triggers all Sly cards in hand. |
| must_pick | Tactician | Sly Energy. Discarded by Acrobatics or Gamble, gives free Energy to keep playing cards in the cycle. |
| high | Reflex | Draws cards when discarded via Sly. Accelerates the cycle without costing Energy. |
| high | Backflip | Draw 2 + Block. Helps fine-tune draw pile count and provides defense. |
| high | Prepared | 0 Energy, draw 1, discard 1. Cheap cycle tool that triggers Sly and adjusts draw pile count. |
| medium | Escape Plan | Draw 1 card and potentially gain Block. Helps adjust draw pile count precisely. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Reflex (`reflex`)
- Backflip (`backflip`)
- Escape Plan (`escape-plan`)
- Survivor (`survivor`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Pendulum** — Draw a card on every shuffle. A cycling deck shuffles constantly, giving extra draws to reach Grand Finale faster.
- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards at the start of combat. Gets the cycle engine started faster on turn 1.
- **Sundial** — Gain Energy every 3 shuffles. The infinite cycle shuffles many times per turn, generating free Energy.

**Good**

- **The Abacus** — Gain Block on shuffle. Provides passive defense while cycling through the deck.
- **Ring of the Snake** — Silent's starter relic. Draw 2 extra cards on turn 1, helping assemble the first cycle faster.
- **White Star** — Helps draw Grand Finale faster, especially in the first few turns before the deck is fully thinned.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Hard cap of 6 cards per turn. The infinite cycle needs to play 10+ cards per turn to function.
- **Cursed Key** — Adding Curses to the deck is catastrophic for a build that needs the smallest possible deck.
- **Snecko Eye** — Randomizing costs breaks the precise Energy calculation needed for the cycle.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Pendulum** — Whenever you shuffle your Draw Pile, draw a card.
- **Bag of Preparation** — At the start of each combat, draw 2 additional cards.

## Silent Shiv Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/silent-shiv-build

Generate waves of Shivs boosted by Accuracy for rapid-fire damage. Each Shiv is a free 0-cost attack that triggers on-attack relics. Fast, aggressive, and deeply satisfying.

### Core cards

- Blade Dance (`blade-dance`)
- Accuracy (`accuracy`)
- Cloak and Dagger (`cloak-and-dagger`)
- Infinite Blades (`infinite-blades`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Accuracy | Every copy adds +4 damage (+6 upgraded) to all Shivs. The single most important card. |
| must_pick | Blade Dance | Generate 3 Shivs (4 upgraded) for 1 Energy. Core Shiv generation. |
| must_pick | Infinite Blades | Generate 1 Shiv at the start of each turn. Free persistent value. |
| high | Cloak and Dagger | Gain 6 Block + 1 Shiv (2 Shivs upgraded). Defense and offense in one card. |
| high | Afterimage | Gain 1 Block for every card played. With 6+ Shivs per turn, that's 6+ free Block. |
| high | Backflip | 5 Block + draw 2 cards. Defensive staple that digs for Accuracy and Shiv generators. |
| medium | Knife Trap | When attacked, generate Shivs. Reactive Shiv generation that punishes enemies for attacking. |
| medium | Finisher | Deals damage per attack played this turn. After playing 6 Shivs, Finisher deals 36+ damage. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Knife Trap (`knife-trap`)
- Fan Of Knives (`fan-of-knives`)
- Storm Of Steel (`storm-of-steel`)
- Finisher (`finisher`)
- Afterimage (`afterimage`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Shuriken** — Gain 1 Strength every 3 attacks played. Shivs are attacks — 6 Shivs per turn = +2 Strength per turn.
- **Kunai** — Gain 1 Dexterity every 3 attacks played. Shivs trigger this constantly, making your Block cards insane.
- **Ring of the Snake** — Draw 2 extra cards turn 1. More cards = more Shiv generators found early.

**Good**

- **Ornamental Fan** — Gain 4 Block every 3 attacks. Shivs trigger this constantly for free passive Block.
- **Pen Nib** — Every 10th attack deals double damage. Count your Shivs to align the 10th hit with a real attack.
- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 more cards at start of combat. Find Blade Dance and Accuracy faster.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — 6 cards per turn limit. Shiv builds want to play 8-12 cards per turn. This kills the build.
- **Snecko Eye** — Random costs on cards. Shivs are free regardless, but your Shiv generators and Accuracy get random costs.
- **Runic Dome** — Not knowing intent means you can't decide between going aggressive with Shivs or blocking.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Shuriken** — Every time you play 3 Attacks in a single turn, gain 1 Strength.
- **Kunai** — Every time you play 3 Attacks in a single turn, gain 1 Dexterity.

### Core cards

- Snakebite (`snakebite`)
- Fan of Knives (`fan-of-knives`)
- Finisher (`finisher`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |

| must_pick | Fan of Knives | Hit ALL enemies + draw a card. With Envenom, applies Poison to everything on screen. |
| must_pick | Finisher | Deal 1 damage per Attack played this turn. Natural payoff for playing many cheap attacks. |
| high | Snakebite | Apply Poison on hit. Works even without Envenom, and stacks with it for double Poison application. |
| high | Blade Dance | Add 3 Shivs to hand. Each Shiv = 1 attack + 1 Poison with Envenom. High value. |
| high | Accuracy | +4 Shiv damage (6 upgraded). Makes Shivs deal real damage alongside Poison application. |
| high | Footwork | Gain 2 Dexterity (3 upgraded). Essential defense for a build focused on offensive pressure. |
| medium | Burst | Play your next Skill twice. Blade Dance x2 = 6 Shivs = 6 Poison + damage. Explosive combo piece. |

- Dagger Spray (`dagger-spray`)
- Accuracy (`accuracy`)

## Silent Combo Execution Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/silent-combo-build

A defensive scaling powerhouse that chains Wraith Form + Nightmare for multiple turns of Intangible, reducing ALL incoming damage to 1. Footwork stacks Dexterity for massive Block, while Burst doubles key skills. Once set up, you become virtually invincible — the ultimate late-game control deck.

### Core cards

- Wraith Form (`wraith-form`)
- Burst (`burst`)
- Nightmare (`nightmare`)
- Footwork (`footwork`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Wraith Form | 2 turns of Intangible (all damage = 1). The build's core defensive card. One copy is enough. |
| must_pick | Nightmare | Add 3 copies of target card to hand next turn. Targeting Wraith Form = 6+ Intangible turns. Game-winning. |
| must_pick | Footwork | Gain 2 Dexterity (3 upgraded). Stack 2-3 copies for 6-9 permanent Dexterity. Every Block card becomes incredible. |
| must_pick | Burst | Play next Skill twice. Burst → Wraith Form = 4 Intangible. Burst → Footwork = double Dexterity. |
| high | Well-Laid Plans | Retain 1 card per turn (2 upgraded). Hold Nightmare until Wraith Form is drawn. Enables the combo. |
| high | Afterimage | Gain 1 Block per card played. Passive defense that rewards playing many cards during safe turns. |
| high | Backflip | 5 Block + draw 2 cards. Efficient defense and cycle to find combo pieces. |
| medium | Adrenaline | Gain 1 Energy, draw 2 cards, Exhaust. Perfect for combo turns when you need extra resources. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Afterimage (`afterimage`)
- Well Laid Plans (`well-laid-plans`)
- Adrenaline (`adrenaline`)
- Backflip (`backflip`)
- Acrobatics (`acrobatics`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Ring of the Snake** — Silent's starter. Extra draw turn 1 helps find combo pieces (Footwork, Wraith Form) faster.
- **Ice Cream** — Energy conserved between turns. Save Energy during Intangible turns to fuel explosive combo plays.
- **Runic Pyramid** — Retain your entire hand between turns. Hold Nightmare until you draw Wraith Form. Perfect synergy.

**Good**

- **Tungsten Rod** — Reduce all HP loss by 1. During Intangible, you already take only 1 — Tungsten Rod makes it 0.
- **Pocketwatch** — Draw 3 extra cards if you played 3 or fewer cards. Setup turns often play few cards, triggering this for draw.
- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards turn 1. Critical for finding Footwork and setup cards early.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Combo turns often require 6+ card plays (Burst + Nightmare + Footwork + Block cards).
- **Philosopher Stone** — Enemies gain Strength. After Intangible wears off, Strength-boosted enemies hit extremely hard.
- **Snecko Eye** — Randomized costs can make Wraith Form unplayable on key turns. The combo needs precise Energy management.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Ring of the Snake** — At the start of each combat, draw 2 additional cards.
- **Ice Cream** — Energy is now conserved between turns.
