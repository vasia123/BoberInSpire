## Regent Forge Build (S-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/regent-forge-build

Power up Sovereign Blade through Forge effects until each swing becomes a virtual apocalypse. The Regent's most consistent and straightforward scaling archetype — Forge your weapon, then destroy everything.

### Core cards

- Seeking Edge (`seeking-edge`)
- Conqueror (`conqueror`)
- Falling Star (`falling-star`)
- Sword Sage (`sword-sage`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Seeking Edge | Premium Forge card. Adds significant permanent damage to Sovereign Blade. Always take. |
| must_pick | Sword Sage | Doubles all Forge gains. Makes every other Forge card twice as effective. Transformative. |
| must_pick | Conqueror | Forge + Stars cost. High Forge value when you have Stars to spend. |
| high | Falling Star | Starting deck card. Deals damage + generates Stars. Upgrade priority. |
| high | Glow | Generate Stars efficiently. Fuel for Forge cards and Star-cost abilities. |
| high | Child of the Stars | Gain Block based on Stars generated. Late-game defensive powerhouse. |
| medium | Heirloom Hammer | Play Sovereign Blade an extra time per turn. Double the value of all your Forge investment. |
| medium | Refine Blade | Cheap Forge card available early. Less powerful than Seeking Edge but easier to find. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Refine Blade (`refine-blade`)
- Hammer Time (`hammer-time`)
- Heirloom Hammer (`heirloom-hammer`)
- Beat into Shape (`beat-into-shape`)
- Child Of The Stars (`child-of-the-stars`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Divine Right** — Regent's starter relic. Generates Stars at the start of combat, fueling Forge cards immediately.
- **Lantern** — Gain 1 extra Energy on turn 1. Crucial for playing Forge cards early.
- **Bag of Marbles** — Apply Vulnerable to all enemies turn 1. Amplifies already-massive Sovereign Blade damage.

**Good**

- **Pen Nib** — Every 10th attack deals double damage. Time this with a fully Forged Sovereign Blade for devastating hits.
- **Vajra** — +1 Strength amplifies Sovereign Blade and all attack damage. Simple and effective.
- **Ornamental Fan** — Gain 4 Block every 3 attacks. Helps survive while Forging in the first few turns.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Forge builds need to play Sovereign Blade + Forge cards + Stars generators. 6-card limit constrains key turns.
- **Ectoplasm** — Gold is essential for buying Forge cards and relics from shops.
- **Runic Dome** — Knowing when to Forge vs when to block is critical. Blind enemy intent makes this impossible.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Divine Right** — At the start of each combat, gain 3 Stars.

## Regent Star Burst Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/regent-star-burst-build

Stockpile massive amounts of Stars, then unleash them all at once through Stardust or Seven Stars for devastating burst damage. The ultimate 'save up and explode' strategy. One of the biggest dopamine hits in the game.

### Core cards

- Stardust (`stardust`)
- Seven Stars (`seven-stars`)
- Glow (`glow`)
- Black Hole (`black-hole`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Stardust | Primary win condition. Deals damage based on Stars spent. 25 Stars = 25+ hits of damage. |
| must_pick | Seven Stars | Alternative finisher. Massive single-hit damage scaling with Star count. |
| must_pick | Black Hole | Passive damage per turn based on Star count. Makes stockpiling itself deal damage. |
| must_pick | Glow | Efficient Star generation. Core engine card — take every copy. |
| high | Child of the Stars | Gain Block based on Stars. Essential defense while building up the burst. |
| high | Gather Light | Generate Stars. Reliable Star source that keeps the engine fueled. |
| medium | Genesis | Reset Energy to max. Enables massive burst turns when spending all Stars. |
| medium | Pillar of Creation | Passive bonus triggered by Star generation. Amplifies the engine. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Gather Light (`gather-light`)
- Child Of The Stars (`child-of-the-stars`)
- Genesis (`genesis`)
- Pillar Of Creation (`pillar-of-creation`)
- Dying Star (`dying-star`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Divine Right** — Regent's starter relic. Free Stars at combat start gives the stockpiling a head start.
- **Lantern** — Extra Energy turn 1 lets you play multiple Star generators immediately.
- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards turn 1. Find Star generators and setup pieces faster.

**Good**

- **Vajra** — +1 Strength amplifies Stardust and Seven Stars damage. Simple multiplicative bonus.
- **Ornamental Fan** — Gain 4 Block every 3 attacks. Passive defense while building Stars.
- **Anchor** — Start with 10 Block. Buys time for the Star accumulation phase.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Burst turns require playing many Star-spending cards. 6-card limit caps your explosive potential.
- **Ectoplasm** — Gold is critical for purchasing key cards like Stardust and Seven Stars from shops.
- **Philosopher Stone** — Enemies gain Strength while you spend 3-4 turns stockpiling. Extra damage compounds dangerously.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Divine Right** — At the start of each combat, gain 3 Stars.
- **Lantern** — Start each combat with an additional Energy.

## Regent Void Form Build (A-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/regent-void-form-build

Bend the rules of resource management with Void Form — end your turn early, but make your first 2 cards free. Combined with Energy conservation and high-value bombs, every turn becomes a surgical strike. The Regent's most cerebral archetype, rewarding perfect card sequencing.

### Core cards

- Void Form (`void-form`)
- Big Bang (`big-bang`)
- Decisions, Decisions (`decisions-decisions`)
- Convergence (`convergence`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Void Form | First 2 cards each turn are free. Unlocks 0-cost Seven Stars, Decisions Decisions, and other expensive bombs. |
| must_pick | Decisions, Decisions | Draw 3, play a Skill 3 times. For free with Void Form. Triples your best Skill each turn. |
| must_pick | Convergence | Retain hand + gain Energy/Star next turn. The perfect setup card for Void Form bomb turns. |
| must_pick | Big Bang | 0 cost: Draw 1, gain 1 Energy, 1 Star, 5 Forge. Exhaust. Maximum efficiency in one card. |
| high | Seven Stars | 7 damage x7 to all enemies. For free via Void Form. One of the best AoE finishers in the game. |
| high | Glow | 1 Energy for 1 Star + draw 2. Efficient Star generation and draw to find bomb cards. |
| high | Comet | 33 damage + 3 Vulnerable + 3 Weak. Free via Void Form. Excellent single-target burst. |
| medium | Cloak of Stars | 1 Star for 7 Block. Cheap defense that doesn't use Energy, preserving it for bigger plays. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Seven Stars (`seven-stars`)
- Glow (`glow`)
- Cloak Of Stars (`cloak-of-stars`)
- Comet (`comet`)
- I Am Invincible (`i-am-invincible`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Divine Right** — Regent's starter. Stars at combat start help fuel early Star-cost cards before Void Form is online.
- **Ice Cream** — Conserve Energy between turns. Save Energy on Convergence turns, spend big on Void Form turns.
- **Lantern** — Extra Energy turn 1. Play Void Form on turn 1 with a Lantern to start the engine immediately.

**Good**

- **Bag of Preparation** — Draw 2 extra cards turn 1. Find Void Form and Convergence faster.
- **Pen Nib** — Every 10th Attack deals double damage. Time this with a free Seven Stars for 98 AoE damage.
- **Vajra** — +1 Strength amplifies all damage cards, especially multi-hit free plays.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Void Form makes 2 cards free but you still want to play more cards per turn. 6-card limit hurts.
- **Ectoplasm** — Gold is essential for purchasing key expensive cards like Seven Stars and Decisions, Decisions.
- **Philosopher Stone** — +1 enemy Strength while you spend turns setting up. Dangerous during the ramp phase.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Divine Right** — At the start of each combat, gain 3 Stars.
- **Ice Cream** — Energy is now conserved between turns.

## Regent Bombardment Build (B-Tier)

**URL:** https://slaythespire-2.com/builds/regent-bombardment-build

Rain cosmic destruction on all enemies. This AoE-focused build uses Bombardment, Meteor Shower, and Gamma Blast to obliterate groups while Knockout Blow handles single targets. The Regent's most aggressive and straightforward archetype — less engine, more explosions.

### Core cards

- Bombardment (`bombardment`)
- Meteor Shower (`meteor-shower`)
- Gamma Blast (`gamma-blast`)
- Knockout Blow (`knockout-blow`)

### Card acquisition priority

| Priority | Card | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| must_pick | Bombardment | AoE damage + Forge for Sovereign Blade. Dual purpose: both direct and scaling damage. |
| must_pick | Meteor Shower | Massive multi-hit AoE. Wrecks groups and deals heavy boss damage with Vulnerable. |
| high | Gamma Blast | Star-cost AoE damage. Converts accumulated Stars into immediate destruction. |
| high | Knockout Blow | 30 damage single-target. Recovers 5 Stars on kill. Premium boss and elite finisher. |
| high | Glow | 1 Star + draw 2. Fuels Star-cost AoE cards while cycling through the deck. |
| medium | Crush Under | Damage + Vulnerable. Sets up Meteor Shower and Bombardment for amplified damage. |
| medium | Bulwark | Strong Block card. Necessary defense between AoE burst turns. |
| medium | Cosmic Indifference | Early game AoE with Stars. Provides consistent clearing before bigger AoE cards come online. |

### Flex / alternative cards

- Cosmic Indifference (`cosmic-indifference`)
- Wrought In War (`wrought-in-war`)
- Crush Under (`crush-under`)
- Glow (`glow`)
- I Am Invincible (`i-am-invincible`)

### Relic priority

**Best**

- **Divine Right** — Regent's starter. Stars fuel Gamma Blast and Star-cost AoE cards immediately.
- **Bag of Marbles** — Apply Vulnerable to all enemies turn 1. Amplifies all your AoE damage significantly.
- **Akabeko** — First Attack each combat deals +8 damage. Powerful with Bombardment or Knockout Blow as openers.

**Good**

- **Vajra** — +1 Strength amplifies all multi-hit attacks. Multiplicative with Meteor Shower's many hits.
- **Strike Dummy** — Striking cards deal +3 damage. Many Regent attack cards benefit from this generic damage boost.
- **Ornamental Fan** — 4 Block every 3 Attacks. Passive defense from playing many attack cards.

**Avoid**

- **Velvet Choker** — Burst turns may require 6+ card plays for maximum AoE output.
- **Snecko Eye** — Randomized costs can make your burst turns unpredictable. The build needs reliable Energy for big attacks.
- **Philosopher Stone** — Enemies gain +1 Strength. Your aggressive build already takes hits — extra enemy damage is dangerous.

### Key relics (sidebar)

- **Divine Right** — At the start of each combat, gain 3 Stars.
- **Bag of Marbles** — At the start of each combat, apply 1 Vulnerable to ALL enemies.
